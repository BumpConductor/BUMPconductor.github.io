Our Mission

We help problem solving organisations find & solve BUMPS


How we think

We are each unique and valuable to our species. We advance by overcoming common obstacles that limit our progress. As our horizon expands we discover an endless supply of ever more complex challenges. These are best addressed by working together.


How we do things

BumpConductor is being built by people with a passion for problem solving. We employ a cross domain approach, leveraging different tools & techniques from across a wide range of deciplines.

We are committed to uncovering better ways of problem solving by doing it and helping others do it.

Throughout this journey we have come to value:

  • Expanding our horizons over remaining within our boundaries
  • Embracing change over accepting the status quo
  • Addressing our needs over indulging in our wants
  • Productive collaboration over unhealthy competition

Where we are

We call problem solving creatures 'Bumpivores' (feed on BUMPS). And Bumpivores are always hungry. BUMPS are borderless and so are we!

BUMPS occur within organisations, across organisations, and throughout our social structures. We'll go wherever needed to devour high-value problems worth solving.

Note that regardless of size, Bumpivores find difficult, complex BUMPS, the tastiest :-)


Like to get involved?

We are always looking for new Bumpivores. If you are passionate about problem solving, and like a challenge, then please contact us.
