Our Mission

To help problem solving organisations find & solve BUMPS


How we think

We are each unique and valuable to our species. We advance by overcoming common obstacles that limit our progress. As our horizon expands we discover an endless supply of ever more complex challenges. These are best addressed by working together.


How we do things

BumpConductor is being built by people with a passion for problem solving.

We pride ourselves on being highly innovative. We continously scout for new (and old) techniques & tools to help us improve our problem solving capabilites.

This includes disciplines as diverse as computing, systems engineering, biology, social sciences, business processes engineering, entrepreneurship...and many more!


Our Values

We see an endless supply of BUMPS to be solved. There is more than enough work to do. With that in mind we love to work with others to create value and solve meaningful problems.

In this age of great change, an increasingly networked world, and an endless supply of work, we are committed to uncovering better ways of problem solving by doing it and helping others do it.

Throughout this journey we have come to value:

  1. Expanding our horizons over remaining within our boundaries
  2. Embracing change over accepting the status quo
  3. Addressing our needs over indulging in our wants
  4. Productive collaboration over unhealthy competition

Where we work

We have a name for a new breed of problem solving creatures. We call them 'Bumpivores' (because they feed on BUMPS). And Bumpivores are always hungry. We like BUMPS both big & small.

BUMPS occur within organisations, across organisations, and throughout our social structures. BUMPS are borderless. We'll go wherever needed to devour high-value problems worth solving.

Note that regardless of size, Bumpivores seem to find the difficult, more complex BUMPS, the tastiest :-)


Like to get involved?

We are building a network of problem solvers and are always looking to connect with hungry Bumpivores who are looking for a challenge.

If you are passionate about meaningful problem solving please contact us.
