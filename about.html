Who we are

We are a startup on mission to .

BumpConductor consultants use simple yet powerful BUMP based process to help strategist visualise, predict and influence the outcome of complex multiplayer issues. Armed with decades of international experience, BumpConductor continues to guide the world’s leading companies through their toughest challenges by developing impactful, and often counterintuitive, strategies that our clients can pursue with confidence. BumpConductor continues to develop its innovative bump based business gaming technology into a well-structured and efficient approach that we call “The BUMPS Based Framework.” The BUMPS Process is highly engaging, interactive and challenges executives to think about business issues differently. When making important decisions, it is typical for businesses to spend a disproportionate amount of time focusing on their own actions and to put too much weight on quantitative analysis. Open Options’ approach is a blending of quantitative and qualitative considerations, using expertly facilitated group discussions to assemble and structure critical organizational knowledge about an important business issue, and then apply powerful game theory software to identify counterintuitive insights and a clear action plan that has full organizational commitment.

What is BumpConductor?

A system for connecting people with high value problems worth solving to people who can solve them. It serves both consumers and businesses.

Who is it for?

Progressive organisations and individuals who want to achieve their goals quicker, cheaper, better, than their competition.

How does it work?

BumpConductor leverages technology and crowd-power to discover, analyse, and distribute problems that matter to members of the BUMPS ecosystem.
