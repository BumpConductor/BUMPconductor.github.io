Dealing with problems in your way

Not all problems need solving!

When we encounter BUMPS we often have choices. Sometimes problems are best solved internally. Sometimes the problem has already been solved by someone somewhere! However that doesn't stop many companies from re-inventing the wheel over and over again - consuming valuable time, money, and resources better ultilised elsewhere. We help you identify the best strategy to follow:

  • Play - try to solve it internally
  • Pay - for existing solutions
  • Pass - try to find a workaround
  • Pray - do nothing, it might just go away!

All approaches may be valid.

Build sustainable momentum

Develop problem solving capabilities

BumpConductor we take a holistic approach to problem solving connecting you to our global ecosystem that can help you on your journey. OpenBump is but one part of a broader initiative which aims to provide a framework, a system, and a collaboration platform to help you and your ecosystem make meaningful impacts to problems worth solving.

  • BUMP Framework - a vision for global problem solving.
  • BUMP System - a methodolgy for generic problem solving based on the framework
  • BUMP Platform - a set of supporting cooperating components that can be used to build tools to support the generic system or specific systems.
  • OpenBump - a key component in the platform
  • Ecosystem - The users of the platform

OpenBump is an opensource project and is committed to opendata. It provides application interfaces (APIs) to support development of custom tools and applications to serve a particular theme, industry sector, or geographical area. We realise that at this point you may be thinking this Bump based initiative is an ambitious undertaking - but we believe in the cause and so we are committed to this undertaking.
