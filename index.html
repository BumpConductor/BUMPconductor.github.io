How Can BumpConductorTM Help You?

BumpConductorTM enables organisations to identify and solve high-value problems.

High-value problems are issues that prevent an organization from reaching its key strategic goals. They may also represent potential for growth, e.g. an underserved market need. Whatever their nature, these high-value problems present a significant opportunity.

But high-value problems can often go unnoticed. They are usually complex and hard to define, and tend to affect organisations in ways that can be difficult to recognise or predict.

BumpConductor will equip your organisation with the means to understand and exploit these opportunties.

BUMPSTM

Our techniques build upon our unique concept of BUMPS (Big Unsolved Meaningful Problems worth Solving)TM. We use this metaphor to visualise, analyse, and communicate complex situations in a way that provides important insights leading to improved decision making.

This fresh approach, used in conjunction with our BUMPS based products and services, enable BumpConductor powered organisations to build and execute powerful strategies in a shorter time frame leading to improved performance.

Techniques

The tools to get things done

Most problem solving techniques are designed to solve problems in isolation and are often used at tactical levels e.g. lean process improvements. We think the time is right for a more ambitious approach. Our unique BUMPS based framework links strategy with execution and can be used to improve problem solving across the organisation. It covers four areas:

  • Destination - Why and Where you're going
  • Direction - How you're going to get there
  • Navigation - What to do about the challenges on the way
  • Drive - Accelerate and sustain momentum

Technology

Boost your business

Expertise and techniques only go so far. We want to take problem solving to new levels by developing a platform for organisations to detect and tackle problems across their value chains. Core components include:

  • BumpDetectorTM - detects problems in given context
  • OpenBUMPSTM - database of high value problems
  • BumpConductorTM - problem solving orchestration & automation
  • Apps - to engage the problem solving community

Network Access

Collaborative Problem Solving

In this age of globalisation we need to be able to collaborate as well as we complete. Our networks may reveal our problems have already been solved somewhere. When encountering BUMPS we see five choices:

  • Play - aim to solve it internally
  • Pause - wait, it may be about to go away
  • Pay - outsource or acquire existing solutions
  • Pass - create a workaround
  • Pray - It's outside our control!

BumpConductor is building a network to maximise re-use of solutions.
