Our Mission

To make critical problems visible, accessible, and actionable.

Why

Critical problems affect our well-being. If not addressed a they result in a clear adverse outcome: dysfunction or death. The one thing we should all do well is Critical Problem Solving.

Problems with Critical Problem Solving

Many individuals and organisations fail to address their critical problems. Sometimes they are not detected. Sometimes they are ignored. Sometimes they fail to solve them. And so on.

We want to improve this by developing the means to help you survive and thrive.

Our Approach

A wise person once said you cannot solve problems using the same thinking used to create them. So we think differently. This led to the creation of the BUMPS Critical Problem Solving Framework. BUMPS (Big Unsolved Meaningful Problems worth Solving)® are thinking blocks to help visualise, analyse, and navigate complex critical problems.

Projects & Consultancy

Your Partners in Problems Solving

Comfortable at strategic and tactical levels we can work across organisations & ecosystems to deliver maximum impact. We can ask and answer questions such as:

  • What are the critical problems?
  • Where are they?
  • How big are they?
  • How did they occur?
  • What to we do about them?
