What's the Problem?

In 2016 the World Economic Forum (WEF) published its Future of Jobs report. The report states:

"A Fourth Industrial Revolution is interacting with other socio-economic and demographic factors to create a perfect storm of business model change in all industries, resulting in major disruptions to labour markets."

The WEF report confirms our own research. It highlights the skills needed to thrive in these changing conditions. Out of the top 10 skills "complex problem solving" is ranked No.1.

But here's the thing. If complex problem solving is the No.1 skill - what big unsolved meaningful problems are we solving?

When we began to ask this question we thought it would be easy to find the answer - but it wasn't!

So we did some research. We discovered that most orgnisations are not consciously problem solving! Furthermore, most individuals and organizations lack complex problem solving capabilites!

But don't just take our word for it. Try these simple tests:

  1. Ask your friends & colleagues what problems are they are solving?
  2. Ask them to name the problems they have previously solved?
  3. Ask them what complex problem solving techniques/tools/apps they are using?
  4. Look the organisations around you. See if you can identify the problems they have solved, the problems they are solving, and the problems they need solving!

What are BUMPS?

BUMPS Big Unsolved Meaningful Problems worth Solving® is both a metaphor and acronym. But it more than a clever use of words. It is the foundation for a new technique. One that can help you think about complex problems in a radically different way.

The BUMPS based complex problem solving approach can help you better visualise, analyse, and communicate complex problem scenarios. Our goal is to help its users create better outcomes!
