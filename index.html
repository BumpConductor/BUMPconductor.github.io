BumpConductor

A system for transforming complex problems into effective solutions.


In 2016 the World Economic Forum (WEF) published its Future of Jobs report. The report states:

"A Fourth Industrial Revolution is interacting with other socio-economic and demographic factors to create a perfect storm of business model change in all industries, resulting in major disruptions to labour markets."

The report highlights the skills needed to thrive in these changing conditions. Out of the top 10 skills "complex problem solving" is ranked No.1.

This WEF report confirms our own research. In addition we have also discovered that most individuals and organizations are lacking key complex problem solving capabilites! BumpConductor is our solution to help close this gap.

The BumpConductor System

The BumpConductor® is a system consisting of a framework, method, and a technology platform. It is based on our theory of BUMPS. BUMPS theory advocates that we can better manage problems by visualising them in complex problem landscape.

What are BUMPS?

BUMPS® is a metaphor we use it in our advance problem solving technique. As an acronym it stands for 'Big Unsolved Meaningful Problems worth Solving'. But it more than a clever use of words. It is the foundations of our theory that can help teams think about complex problems in a radically different way.

The BUMPS based complex problem solving approach can help you better visualise, analyse, and communicate complex problem scenarios. Our goal is to help its users create better outcomes!
