Why BumpConductorTM

BumpConductorTM is building the means to detect & capture high-value problems worth solving for consumption by problem solving organisations.

High-value problems are issues that prevent an individual or organization from reaching their key strategic goals. They may also represent potential for growth, e.g. an underserved market need. Whatever their nature, these high-value problems present a significant opportunity.

But high-value problems can often go unnoticed. They are usually complex and hard to define, and tend to affect people in ways that can be difficult to recognise or predict.

BumpConductor will equip problem solving organisations with the means to identify, understand, and exploit these opportunties.

BUMPSTM

Our techniques build upon our unique concept of BUMPS (Big Unsolved Meaningful Problems worth Solving)TM. We use this metaphor to visualise, analyse, and communicate complex situations in a way that provides important insights leading to improved decision making.

This fresh approach, used in conjunction with our BUMPS based products and services, enable BumpConductor powered organisations to build and execute powerful strategies in a shorter time frame leading to improved performance.

Techniques

The tools to get things done

Most problem solving techniques are designed to solve problems in isolation and are often used at tactical levels e.g. lean process improvements. We think the time is right for a more ambitious approach. Our unique BUMPS based framework links strategy with execution and helps align an organisation. It covers four areas:

  • Destination - Where you want to go
  • Purpose - Why you are going there
  • Course - How you're going to get there
  • Navigation - What to do about the challenges on the way

Technology

Boost your business

Expertise and techniques only go so far. We want to take problem solving to new levels. We are developing a platform for organisations to detect and tackle problems across their value chains. Core components include:

  • BumpDetectorTM - detects problems in given context
  • OpenBUMPSTM - database of high value problems
  • BumpConductorTM - problem solving orchestration & automation
  • Apps - to engage the problem solving community

Network

Collaborative Problem Solving

In this age of globalisation we need to collaborate as well as we complete. When encountering BUMPS we see five choices:

  • Play - create a new solution
  • Pay - outsource or acquire existing solutions
  • Pass - create a workaround
  • Pause - wait, it may be about to go away
  • Pray - It's outside our control!

Powerful networks help reveal problems that have already been solved somewhere. BumpConductor is building a network to maximise re-use of solutions.
