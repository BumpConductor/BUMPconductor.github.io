How Can BumpConductor Help You?

BumpConductorTM helps organisations identify and address the high value obstacles impacting their goals.

We provide a highly visual approach to strategy and delivery based on the concept of BUMPS (Big Unsolved Meaningful Problems worth Solving)TM. This metaphor helps us visualise, analyse, and communicate complex situations in a way that provides important insights leading to improved decision making and organisational alignment.

This fresh approach, combined with our unique products and services, enable BumpConductor powered organisations to build and execute powerful strategies in a short time frame leading to improved performance.

Techniques

The tools to get things done

Traditional problem solving techniques are often designed to solve a specific problem and are often used at tactical levels e.g. lean process improvements. We think the time is right for a more ambitious approach. Our unique BUMPS based problem solving framework aims to improve problem solving capabilities at both strategic and tactical levels. It is based on four core areas:

  • Destination - Why and Where your going
  • Direction - How your going to get there
  • Navigation - What you need to do along the way
  • Drive - Accelerate and sustain momentum

Technology

Tools to boost business

Expertise and techniques only go so far. We are developing the means to take problem solving to a new level. We are working on a technology platform to able organisations to detect and tackle problems quickly and effectively. Although this is long term project our current initiatives include:

  • BumpDetectorTM - detects problems in social/business sectors
  • OpenBUMPSTM - database of high value problems
  • BumpConductorTM - problem solving orchestration & automation
  • Apps - to engage the problem solving community
