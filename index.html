Join High Impact EcoSystems

Collaborative Problem Solving

OpenBump provides the means to locate and connect the key actors necessary to reach effective outcomes. We actively seek out and connect new and emerging eco-system players worldwide including:

  • for-profit, not-for-profit enterprises, startups
  • government bodies, regulators, industry associations
  • educators, accelerators, incubators
  • investors, doners, foundations
  • analysts, investigators, journalists,
  • campaigners, activists, and more

OpenBump seeks to understand who you are and what your doing. Through OpenBump you can promote your profile and achievements to a global ecosystem and connect with others that can help you deliver an impact.

Make Impact

Tools & Techniques to get things done!

OpenBump is but one part of a broader initiative which aims to provide a framework, a system, and a collaboration platform to help you and your ecosystem make meaningful impacts to problems worth solving.

  • BUMP Framework - a vision for global problem solving.
  • BUMP System - a methodolgy for generic problem solving based on the framework
  • BUMP Platform - a set of supporting cooperating components that can be used to build tools to support the generic system or specific systems.
  • OpenBump - a key component in the platform
  • Ecosystem - The users of the platform

OpenBump is an opensource project and is committed to opendata. It provides application interfaces (APIs) to support development of custom tools and applications to serve a particular theme, industry sector, or geographical area. We realise that at this point you may be thinking this Bump based initiative is an ambitious undertaking - but we believe in the cause and so we are committed to this undertaking.
