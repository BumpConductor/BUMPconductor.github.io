How Can BumpConductor Help You?

BumpConductorTM helps leading organisations identify and address the high value obstacles standing between them and their goals.

We do this by creating unique solutions based around the concept of BUMPS (Big Unsolved Problems worth Solving)TM. This metaphor helps us visualise, analyse, and communicate obstacles in a way that provides important insights leading to improved decision making.

Our fresh approach, combined with powerful techniques & tools, enable BumpConductor to effectively align organisations with strategy in a short time frame leading to improved performance.

The BUMPSTM Problem Solving Framework

Our BUMPS framework creates a useful set of reference points and building blocks to guide us in our problem solving journey from strategy to execution. It covers four areas:

  • Destination - Decide where you're going
  • Direction - Decide the best route to get you there
  • Navigation - Decide how to deal with the obstacles in your way
  • Drive - Accelerate and sustain momentum

Destination

Decide where you're going

Established organisations often view the world through a narrow lens. Often they will remain focused on existing products & services, resulting in missed opportunites addressing high value market BUMPS. We develop tools & techniques to help organisations view the world differently. An example of this BumpDetector, a marketing strategy tool being built identify high value problems worth solving. It will help our partners answer the following questions:

  • What are the most valuable problems?
  • Where are they?
  • How big are they?
  • Who is the addressible market?

Direction

Determine the best route

Although many organisations may be heading for similar goals they start their journey from different places and with very different approaches. Whatever your starting point you need to set a course and prepare for the challenges you will encounter. We understand this journey and are able to apply our BUMPS framework to help you identify, analyse, and strategize the best route to take. We can help:

  • Define and Refine your goals
  • Identify and map the challenges
  • Generate and test your strategy

Navigation

Decide how to deal with the obstacles in your way

When we encounter BUMPS we often have choices. Sometimes problems are related to a core capability and are best solved internally. Often the problem doesn't need to be solved internally and can be outsourced. Often a similar problem has already been solved by someone somewhere! However that doesn't stop many companies from re-inventing the wheel over and over again - consuming valuable time, money, and resources better ultilised elsewhere. We help you analyse the problems and make good decisions e.g.

  • Play - try to solve it internally
  • Pay - outsource or acquire existing solutions
  • Pass - create a workaround
  • Pray - do nothing, it might just go away!

Drive

Accelerate and sustain momentum

Successful strategy implementation will give any organisation a significant competative advantage. But data shows that many struggle to achieve acceptable levels of success. Indeed, successful execution can be said to be BUMPS for most organisations, and often the root causes are the same e.g. due to a lack of buy-in or understanding, resulting in a resistance to change.

BumpConductor helps address this problem by creating tools and techniques to help align people to common organisational goals, and ultimately developing a culture that embraces problem solving as a key part of its DNA.
